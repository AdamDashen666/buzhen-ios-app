Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Buzhen Local does not require an account and does not collect personal information from users.

Videos and files selected by the user are processed locally on the device. The app does not upload user videos, images, audio, or exported files to our servers.

Buzhen Local does not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics SDKs, or user behavior tracking.

We do not sell, rent, or share user personal information with third parties.

The app may request access to files or media only so the user can select videos for local processing and save/export results. These permissions are used only for app functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

Buzhen Local does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

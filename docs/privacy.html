Privacy Policy for Buzhen

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Buzhen is a local video frame interpolation and video processing app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, how local files are handled, and how users can contact the developer.

1. Data Collection

Buzhen does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal data with the developer or with third parties.

The app does not use:

  • Third-party analytics
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • User accounts or sign-in systems
  • Remote servers for video processing

2. Video, Photo, and File Access

Buzhen may ask for access to videos, photos, or files only when you choose to import media for processing or export a processed result.

Your videos and files are processed locally on your device. They are not uploaded to the developer, to cloud servers, or to third-party services by the app.

3. Processing Logs

Buzhen may generate local processing information such as progress, frame rate, CPU usage, GPU estimate, memory usage, processing mode, target FPS, quality setting, bitrate, and thermal status. This information is used only to show progress and help you understand processing performance.

Processing logs remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

4. Tracking

Buzhen does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. The app does not create advertising profiles and does not share data for advertising or tracking purposes.

5. Children’s Privacy

Buzhen does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data, no personal data from children is stored or shared by the developer.

6. Data Retention

Since Buzhen does not collect personal data, the developer does not retain personal data. Media files, exported videos, and exported logs are stored only where you choose to save them on your device or in your own storage locations.

7. Security

Buzhen is designed to minimize data exposure by processing videos locally on device. You should still protect your device, iCloud account, and exported files using your normal device security settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change. Any update will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

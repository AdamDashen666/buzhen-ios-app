Common Help

  • Import a video from Files or Photos.
  • Choose target FPS, quality, bitrate, and processing mode.
  • Keep the app open during processing for best reliability.
  • Export the processed video after completion.
  • Use the log export option to save processing details if you need help troubleshooting.

Privacy

Buzhen processes videos locally on your device and does not upload your media to the developer. Read the Privacy Policy.
